Welcome, to the life of Travis Scott

A short summary of Travis Scotts upbringing and musical career.

His Backstory:

Jauqes Bermon Webster was born in Houston Texas, in 1991. He started making music professionally under the alias Travis Scott in 2013 with his first mixtape "Owl Pharoah". After that, he went on to release another mixtape before his debut album "Rodeo" After his debut album recieved positive reviews and he gained popularity, he went on to release 3 more albums and solidified himself as one of rap musics greats.
